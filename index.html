Meet Chef Ritu Godha

A gifted culinary artist whose heart and soul lie in the comforting warmth of her kitchen. A passionate baker by nature, Ritu has spent years perfecting her craft—turning flour, sugar, and a sprinkle of love into beautiful, decadent desserts that not only taste incredible but also look like works of art. Her cakes rise like poetry, her cookies melt in your mouth, and her breads carry the comforting aroma of home.

Her talents don’t stop at baking. Ritu is also a master of vegetarian cuisine, creating dishes that are vibrant, flavorful, and deeply nourishing. Whether she’s preparing a creamy vegetable korma, a perfectly spiced dal, or a fresh and colorful salad bursting with seasonal ingredients, Ritu approaches every dish with creativity, care, and a deep respect for wholesome, plant-based cooking. She believes that food is more than sustenance—it’s a way to connect, to heal, and to celebrate life. In Ritu’s kitchen, every ingredient has a purpose, every recipe has a story, and every meal is made with passion. Whether she’s whipping up a tray of cinnamon rolls for her family or preparing an elaborate vegetarian feast for friends, Ritu brings people together through her love of food, one delicious bite at a time.

Pricing & Terms of Service

  • ✔️ We are a 100% Vegetarian Business. No eggs are used. Vegan options are available.
  • ✔️ The entire menu is pre-order based. Not a storefront — the earliest an order would be ready is the next day.
  • ✔️ Prices include cake, filling, icing, and decoration. Prices do not include toppers, ornaments, or delivery.
  • ✔️ A 50% deposit is required to place and schedule an order. Full payment is due before pickup or delivery.
  • ✔️ Cake stands are available for a rental fee and must be returned clean and undamaged within 3 days of the event.
